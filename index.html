• Chocolate Bread

    • How to make a Chocolate bread.

    • First,bring flour,cocoa flour, Sugar,Egg, and milk
      • Second, put flour, milk, egg in bowl. Third, mix little and put some sugar and cocoa flour. Fourth, when dough got a shape, pit some oil on the hand. Third, make any shape and put on paper foil. Sixth, bake 20min in 140*. Seventh, put cocoa flour and milk make a syrup. Last, when bake is finish put syrup inside of bread and enjoy it.
